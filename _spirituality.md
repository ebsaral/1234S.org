# Spirituality

**Spirituality** is how human beings understand themselves, their place in the universe, and their connection to something greater than the material world. It is largely shaped as a personal journey, unfolding uniquely throughout each person's lifetime.

## Core Values of Spirituality

Though diverse, spiritual traditions often converge on universal values:

- Justice
- Equality
- Freedom
- Truth
- Compassion
- Love
- Forgiveness
